Game Rules

Welcome to the Slots Game! The objective is to spin the reels and match symbols to win prizes.

  • Click the SPIN button to spin the reels.
  • Adjust your bet amount using the "+" and "-" buttons.
  • If all three middle symbols match, you win according to the payout table.
  • Payouts vary based on the symbol and your bet amount.
