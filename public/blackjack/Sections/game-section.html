Game Rules

1. Try to get as close to 21 without going over.

2. Each player starts with two cards, and the dealer has one card face down.

3. Aces can count as either 1 or 11, depending on which value benefits your hand.

4. If your first two cards are of the same value, you can split them into two hands.

Button Actions

Hit: Request another card to try to improve your hand total.

Double: Double your initial bet and receive only one additional card.

Stand: Keep your current hand and end your turn.

Split: If dealt two cards of the same value, split them into two separate hands.
